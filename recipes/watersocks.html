RiverSocks

It's free, it's easy and it's free

Ingredients

  • One sock
  • Another sock
  • A river
  • Something to capture and pour water

Instructions

  1. Get your socks off
  2. Go to your local river and capture some water
  3. Pour the captured water into your socks
  4. Put your socks back on
  5. Profit

Easy, fast and free
